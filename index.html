ABOUT ME



Hey there, I'm Ananya, a passionate frontend web developer and photographer with a knack for bringing creativity to life through pixels and pixels captured through the lens. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, I craft stunning and responsive web experiences that engage and captivate users.I'm currently pursuing B.Tech in Electrical Engineering in NIT Silchar. As a photographer, I don't just capture moments; I weave stories through the lens. Whether it's the interplay of light and shadows, the beauty of candid shots, or the meticulous planning of a photoshoot, photography is my canvas to paint emotions and narratives. I find inspiration in the ordinary, transforming everyday scenes into extraordinary visual tales. So, whether I'm crafting elegant web interfaces, capturing the essence of moments through photography, or delving into the intricacies of programming, I'm dedicated to pushing boundaries and creating meaningful connections between technology, art, and people. Feel free to reach out if you want to collaborate, discuss creative ideas, or simply share your love for the fascinating world we live in!
